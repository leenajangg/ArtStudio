Meet the Artist: Leena Jang

Hi my name is Leena Jang. I am currently a sophomore studying graphic design and advertising at Boston University. With my passion and talent in art, I've been pursuing towards my art career in the fine art and further seeking education in graphic design. Besides art, I'm also passionate about learning and sharing with others about my cultural heritage, working with people, leading people, helping out others, and giving back to the community.


Meet the Artist: Sarah Nam

Hello my name is Sarah Nam, and I am a sophomore at Boston University, studying as a graphic design major. I have always been interested in the fine arts, and being able to pursue it and the finer intricacies of design is a dream come true for me. My art generally stems from things that I feel influence me, or things that represent me. Most of my pieces showcase my thoughts about myself and the emotions that I feel towards various subject matters.
